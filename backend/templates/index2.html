Steps to Upload Curriculum
and Generate Question Bank

01

Upload Curriculum

Click the "Upload" button.

Select the PDF file that contains the curriculum to be used for generating questions.

02

Set Parameters & Generate Questions

03

Select Recipients & Send

Select/Enter the email of the recipients by checking the boxes.

Click the "Send" button to email the generated question bank PDF to the chosen recipients.

Generate Question Bank

The Trainer UI of the Automated Question Builder Application facilitates seamless curriculum management and question bank creation for educators. The interface allows trainers to review and edit generated questions before finalizing them. Additionally, a feedback mechanism helps improve the question banks over time. This user-friendly design ensures that trainers can efficiently create and manage assessments, enhancing the overall learning experience.

Unlocking Opportunities for Continuous Learning


Stay on top of your self-assessment and continue to grow your skills with personalized question banks tailored just for you.


Explore

Role Description

Employees utilize the Automated Question Builder Application to access question banks for self-assessment and training. They can request specific question sets based on their learning needs, enabling them to enhance their skills and knowledge in various technologies.

Employee Dashboard

Steps to Generate Your Self-Assessment Quiz

01

Click on Self-Assessment

To begin the process, navigate to the self-assessment section of the platform. By clicking on the designated "Self-Assessment" button, the quiz panel will open up, providing you with access to various quizzes tailored to different subjects.

02

Enter the Subject with Difficulty Level and Number of Questions

Once the quiz panel is open, you will need to specify the subject you want to assess yourself on. Additionally, select the difficulty level (easy, medium, or hard) that best fits your current knowledge. Finally, indicate how many questions you would like to attempt in the quiz. This customization ensures that the quiz is aligned with your learning needs and preferences.

03

Start the Quiz Finally

After setting your preferences, it’s time to start the quiz! Click the “Start Quiz” button to begin answering the questions. Ensure you read each question carefully and submit your answers. Your results will be evaluated at the end, providing insights into your understanding of the subject matter.

Self-Assessment

Steps to Complete Assessment by Trainer

01

Click on Assessment by Trainer

To initiate the assessment process, click on the "Assessment by Trainer" button on the platform. This will take you to the assessment section where you can view the available assessments set by your trainer.

02

Attend the MCQs Provided by the Trainer

After selecting the assessment, you will find multiple-choice questions (MCQs) prepared by your trainer. Take your time to read each question carefully and select the answer you believe is correct. Ensure you complete all the questions within the allotted time frame.

03

Reports Will Be Generated

Once you have submitted your answers, a report will be generated summarizing your performance in the assessment. This report will help you identify areas of strength and opportunities for improvement based on your responses.

Steps to Generate Your Customized Timetable

01

Click on Customize Timetable Button

To initiate the process of creating your personalized timetable, locate and click on the "Customize Timetable" button. This will direct you to the section where you can upload your syllabus and specify your scheduling preferences.

02

Upload Your Syllabus

After clicking the button, you will be prompted to upload your syllabus file. Ensure that the syllabus is in an accepted format (such as PDF or DOCX) and includes all the necessary details for the modules. This information is crucial for generating an accurate and personalized timetable.

03

Personalized Module-wise Schedule Will Be Generated

Once your syllabus is uploaded, our system will automatically generate a personalized module-wise schedule for you. This timetable will outline your classes, study sessions, and any other important deadlines, helping you manage your time effectively.
